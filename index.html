02 — Selected Projects
Things I've Built
AI-Powered Legal Document Analysis Research · Production View Project
+

84.9% F1-score on 510 legal contracts · Presented at NCRIE-2025

React.js Node.js BERT Python OpenAI API

Problem

Legal teams spend hours manually reviewing contracts for risk clauses — no consistency, no audit trail, no scale.

What I Built

Full-stack MERN app for real-time AI-driven clause extraction from PDFs and Word documents. Fine-tuned BERT with OpenAI API to produce plain-language risk summaries. Trust & Safety module includes PII redaction and live fairness auditing.

Key Engineering Decision

Fine-tuned BERT on the CUAD benchmark instead of relying on prompt engineering alone — because 84.9% F1 is a number you can defend in a paper and in production. Measurable over impressive.

Result

84.9% F1, 84.7% accuracy on CUAD (510 legal contracts). Research paper presented at NCRIE-2025, KSIT — Best Paper Presentation Award.
